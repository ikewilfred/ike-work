P.P.E REQUIREMENT

While the equipment is
necessary for both public and personal health,
there's one glaring issue in the US right now concerning PPE: a major shortage and its putting any one who comes
into contact with COVID-19 patients at risk

Dr. Pischel echoes this: “As the world has been treating cases from this pandemic,
a lot of PPE has been used up. This creates a great stain on
supply chains with resulting significant shortages of PPE with different regions
impacted more severely than others,” she explains. “Many health systems are starting to ration PPE
because this pandemic will likely go on for some time so we need to save PPE
for several weeks to months for now in order to avoid a situation where there
is no PPE at all.
